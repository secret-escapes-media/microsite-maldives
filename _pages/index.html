The Maldives

intro: >

An ecological paradise and the standard-bearer for luxury, the Maldives is a destination that simply defies belief. Scattered across the Indian Ocean in remote atolls, this island nation is not only a wonderland for diving and sailing, but a place rich in culture and passionate about conservation. Digs range from low-slung bungalows that let the views do the talking, to sprawling over-water villas that give the fantasy scenery a run for its money.
